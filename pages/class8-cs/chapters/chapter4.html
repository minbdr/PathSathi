Chapter 4: Cyber Law and Computer Ethics

Understanding Cyber Law

Cyber law refers to the legal framework that governs the use of computers, networks, and the internet. It addresses issues related to cybercrimes, data protection, and digital rights.

Computer Ethics

Computer ethics deals with moral principles and guidelines for responsible use of computer technology.

Key Topics

  • Introduction to cyber law and its importance
  • Types of cybercrimes and their legal consequences
  • Intellectual property rights in digital world
  • Privacy and data protection laws
  • Ethical use of computers and internet
  • Digital citizenship and responsibilities
  • Software piracy and copyright issues
